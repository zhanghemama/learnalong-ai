# 7-Day Self-Use Validation

The goal is not to prove that the prototype has many features. The goal is to prove that it fits a real learning habit.

## Daily Use

1. Open one AI-related video, article, X thread, or doc page.
2. Ask at least one question by voice.
3. Say “记一下” when something feels worth keeping.
4. Generate one HTML learning article after a meaningful session.
5. Reopen the article the next day and see whether it is still useful.

## Metrics

- Active days: used on at least 5 of 7 days.
- Voice questions: at least 20 total questions in a week.
- Saved notes: at least 5 notes that still feel useful the next day.
- Generated articles: at least 3 articles worth rereading.
- Friction moments: any moment where the user wanted to ask but did not.

## Decision Rules

```text
Worth continuing:
  active days >= 5
  voice questions >= 20
  generated articles worth rereading >= 3

Promising:
  active days >= 3
  voice questions >= 8
  at least one article feels worth sharing

Not yet proven:
  opened only for demos
  generated articles feel generic
  voice asking feels slower than typing into ChatGPT
```

## Most Important Question

Ask this after every session:

```text
Did LearnAlong AI help me understand something I would otherwise skip?
```

If the answer is repeatedly yes, the next step is cloud distribution or browser store packaging. If the answer is no, improve context tracking and article quality before adding integrations.
